## Executive Summary

This presentation outlines the implementation and impact of an automated NFA (Nota Fiscal Avulsa) processing system that has achieved **87.8% reduction in processing time** while maintaining **100% accuracy**. The system represents a significant advancement in public service efficiency, reducing taxpayer wait times and improving service quality.

### Key Achievements

- ✅ **87.8% time reduction** per transaction (9.0 min → 1.10 min)
- ✅ **100% accuracy** maintained (vs. 94.2% manual accuracy)
- ✅ **Zero fatigue-related errors** (vs. 5.8% human error rate)
- ✅ **8.18x processing capacity** increase
- ✅ **Taxpayer service improvement** through faster response times
